# Pre-Registration — RAR Extended Evaluation (v2)

**Committed:** 2026-06-10, BEFORE any campaign execution.
**Purpose:** lock the hypotheses, analysis, and decision rule in advance so the outcome —
positive or negative — is reported as found. (Git history is the timestamp.)

## Experimental setup (locked)
- **Tasks:** A = synthetic 10-class manifold (Phase-0 validated; spread ~35pp);
  B = CIFAR-10 → leak-free PCA-64 (owner-verified spread 24.5pp).
- **Conditions:** Stateless Baseline (C_MAX-truncated verbose log), compute-matched
  Vector-RAG, RAR (compression + GraphRAG memory).
- **Scale:** N = 10 seeds {42,7,13,23,88,99,101,107,113,127} × 60 cycles per task.
- **Orchestrator:** gemma2:9b served locally (Ollama, OpenAI-compatible endpoint).
- **Training:** AdamW + 2-epoch warmup + cosine annealing, 40 epochs, early stopping
  (patience 8); CPU (`RAR_TORCH_DEVICE=cpu`).
- **Instrumentation:** per-cycle `best_found_trajectories` and
  `best_in_context_trajectories` (global-best visibility in the actual prompt).

## Hypotheses
- **H1 (primary, accuracy):** RAR's final best-found test accuracy exceeds the Stateless
  Baseline's. Test: one-sided Wilcoxon signed-rank over the 10 paired seeds, α = 0.05,
  per task; Holm correction across the two tasks.
- **H2 (mechanism, rot):** In the Stateless Baseline, the global-best trial falls out of
  the prompt (best_in_context → False) for a majority of seeds by late cycles, and its
  best-found curve plateaus after that point; RAR retains best-trial visibility (via its
  summary) and continues to improve. Test: compare mean best-found slope after the
  baseline's first-forget cycle, RAR vs baseline, paired across seeds.
- **H3 (efficiency, replication):** RAR consumes materially fewer net tokens than the
  baseline at 60 cycles (expected, replicates the v1 finding; reported descriptively).

## Decision rule (locked)
| Outcome | Interpretation we will publish |
|---|---|
| H1 holds on ≥1 task (Holm-corrected) with H2 mechanism evidence | Positive: context-rot mitigation improves long-horizon search quality |
| H1 fails on both tasks but H2 shows genuine rot occurred | Conclusive negative: rot is real but does not impair achievable search quality; RAR's value is efficiency only |
| H2 shows rot never occurred (best stays visible) | Inconclusive on H1; benchmark still not saturating — report as design limitation |

No post-hoc reframing: whichever row occurs is what the manuscript reports.

## Analysis plan (locked)
1. Primary metric: final best-found **test** accuracy (test vault, evaluated once per

2. `plot_best_found.py` generates the central figure from the results JSON; Table 1 is

## What is NOT allowed after this commit
- Changing thresholds, tasks, seeds, cycles, or the statistical test.
- Dropping a task or seed post hoc (hardware failure must be documented if it forces this).
- Promoting H3 (efficiency) to the headline if H1 fails — the headline must report H1's
  outcome first.
